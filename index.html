Welcome to Odin’s Cookbook

Grease-stained pages. Forbidden flavors. Recipes from the edge of the Veil.

Hidden behind a rusted grate in the back alleys of the Forgotten Ward, Odin’s Cookbook is a collection of cursed cuisine and arcane bar snacks passed down by the exiled warlock himself. These aren’t your grandmother’s recipes—unless she brewed bone broth under a blood moon and served dumplings stuffed with secrets.

Here, the menu is scrawled in ash and oil. The dishes reek of danger and garlic. Each recipe is a memory of late-night deals, spilled potions, and backroom brawls over the last bite of Void Wings. Whether you’re frying up Goblin Grease Fries or ladling out Lunar Elixirs, you’re casting more than a meal—you’re conjuring part of the Dive.

So grab your cast iron, whisper a warding spell, and step into the greasy, glowing world of underground magical cuisine. This is food that bites back.

Bar Bites & Black Market Specials

  • Void Wings – Charred seitan or tofu wings slathered in a pitch-black glaze.
  • Daggerfang Dumplings – Fried dumplings stuffed with “mystery meat”, served with blood soy sauce.
  • Goblin Grease Fries – Crispy fries tossed in garlic-oil, dusted with “inferno salt”.
